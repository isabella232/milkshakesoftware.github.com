One of the things that brought the most comfort and inspiration was the organic cohort of other newsroom moms — anchors, reporters, producers, editors in the trenches, juggling motherhood and daily pressures with equal measure and determination. Watching them chase stories that helped make their communities better or finding ways to pump in live trucks was inspiring. Having them a desk away to talk quietly when a child was sick or to celebrate milestones was life-saving. Seek out other newsroom moms who’ve faced and understand the unique challenges you’re facing. They will save you, too.
